FREE Workshop: Maintaining
fitness while working from home

Working from home has disrupted the routine for many of us. Since this is becoming a new normal way of working and is likely to continue even after the things go back to normal, we need to rework our routine and habits for this new reality.

Join me in this FREE virtual workshop and learn how you can:
1. Create your routine to maintain mental and physical fitness while working from home
2. Set expectations in both professional and personal areas
3. Take specific steps to prevent burnout 4. Plan your nutrition requirement during the day
